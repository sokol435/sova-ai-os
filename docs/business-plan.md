# SOVA AI Business Plan

SOVA is an AI-powered automated business system.

The goal is to build a scalable online business using artificial intelligence for:

- product research
- mystery box creation
- marketing automation
- sales optimization
- customer support
- data analysis

This repository will serve as the central brain of the SOVA AI Operating System.

The system will evolve over time and automate most business operations while the owner focuses on strategic decisions.
