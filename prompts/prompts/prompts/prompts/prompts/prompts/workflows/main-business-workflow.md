# SOVA Main Business Workflow

## Purpose
This workflow defines how the SOVA AI system operates from idea to sale.

## Step 1: Goal Definition
- CEO Agent defines the business goal
- Master Orchestrator assigns tasks

## Step 2: Market Research
- Research Agent finds product ideas
- Competitor Agent analyzes competitors
- Trend analysis is reviewed

## Step 3: Offer Creation
- Mystery Box Agent creates the box concept
- Pricing Agent suggests pricing
- Sales Agent improves the offer

## Step 4: Marketing Preparation
- Marketing Agent creates campaign ideas
- Content agents generate posts, ads, and scripts
- Platform selection is decided

## Step 5: Launch
- Product goes live
- Marketing content is published
- Traffic starts coming in

## Step 6: Customer Handling
- Customer Support Agent answers questions
- Order handling system tracks sales
- Feedback is collected

## Step 7: Optimization
- Data Analyst reviews performance
- Ads Optimizer improves campaigns
- CEO Agent decides what to scale

## Final Goal
Create a repeatable AI-driven business process that grows over time.
